About the project

Guided by the common mission "To inspire change in Bulgarian education", TeachMe and MENSA Bulgaria joined forces in building a smarter generation. Our main goal is to support the overall development of students who will build a better future for Bulgaria.

Recognizing the main shortcomings in the curriculum in modern Bulgarian education, namely the focus on acquiring and developing next generation skills, TeachMe has developed special IQ courses for students throughout the country under the patronage of MENSA Bulgaria.

During the training the students will learn interesting techniques for solving problems, will encounter intriguing life situations and business cases, which will develop the key skills for modern life, namely logical and analytical thinking, problem solving skills. With the help of logic puzzles and riddles, they will develop attention to detail, learn how to generate ideas, how to assess risk in different situations and, of course, how to work in a team.

Each school has the opportunity to integrate the course into its educational program. Together we will help Bulgarian children develop all the key skills they need today to succeed tomorrow! Let's remove the Trouble in her work as a teacher!
